Make your front-end

Analytics and automation tools driven by AI that help you analyze and quickly fix inconsistencies, inefficiencies, and dead code in your UI.

Component Analytics

Radien finds all of your components, categorizes them, and finds inefficiencies that can be addressed. Use the Review and Replace feature to clean up duplicates in seconds and without opening your code edtior.

//More consistent

Variation Audits

Radien can compare repos to find variations between them allowing your team to comply better with design systems or design libraries, for example.

//More compliant

Track Compliance

Radien tracks how your repos are changing over time and can help your team quickly grasp how well the repo is adhering to a design system.
